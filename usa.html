A Steak of Sweet Freedom

Steak is amazing.Period. But if you want to take it to the next level, add a little bittle bit of freedom to it!

Ingredient List

  • Your favorite cut of steak

So how do you make it? Just Follow the easy steps below!

  1. Go to the store and purchase your favorite cut of steak
  2. Take the steak and bring it up to room temperature
  3. Cut the steak into the obvious shape of freedom
  4. Heat up a pan and sear that freedom meat on both sides
  5. Eat up!
