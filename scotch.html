Scotch Whiskey

If there is one recipe that i always live by it's Scotch!

Ingredient List

  • Your favorite scotch whiskey or multiple kinds!

So how do you make it? Just Follow the easy steps below!

  1. Go to the store and purchase a bottle of scotch
  2. Go home and find yourself a nice glass and a comfortable chair
  3. Place no more than 3 cubes of ice (or none at all) into the glass
  4. Pour scotch into glass and consume at will
  5. Repeat as necessary
